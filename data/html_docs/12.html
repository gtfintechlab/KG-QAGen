364-DAY REVOLVING CREDIT AGREEMENT

This 364-Day Revolving Credit Agreement, dated as of January 18, 2019, is among WALGREENS BOOTS ALLIANCE, INC., a Delaware corporation (the Borrower), the institutions from time to time parties hereto as Lenders (whether by execution of this Agreement or an assignment pursuant to Section 12.01) and MIZUHO BANK, LTD., as Administrative Agent.
